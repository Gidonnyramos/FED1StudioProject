Images

Implementing images in a website can make content more engaging and sometimes deliver information in a more concise way. There are several different image file formats that are widely used in web development.

JPG or JPEG

Characteristics

  • Highly compressed
  • Ideal for images with many complex colors (ie photos)
  • Stands for Joint Photographic Experts Group
  • Lossy Compression: making changes and saving over the same file will result in degraded image quality.
  • DO NOT support animation
  • DO NOT support transparency

GIF

Characteristics

  • ideal for line art, illustrations, or images with few complex colors
  • Lossless Compression: No quality deterioration
  • They support basic animation
  • They support transparency

PNG

Characteristics

  • Lossless Compression: no quality deterioration
  • DO NOT support animation
  • They support transparency
